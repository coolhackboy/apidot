## The Pricing Models: Per-Second vs Flat Rate

The fundamental difference between these two platforms lies in their pricing approach:

- **Fal.ai** uses a **per-second pricing model**, charging based on video duration
- **Poyo.ai** offers a **flat-rate pricing model**, charging a fixed price per video regardless of length (within limits)

This distinction becomes crucial when calculating costs at scale.

## Why Flat-Rate Pricing Matters

### 1. Predictable Costs
With per-second pricing, your monthly costs can vary dramatically based on video duration. Flat-rate pricing provides predictable budgeting for businesses.

### 2. No Duration Anxiety
When paying per second, there's a natural tendency to keep videos shorter than optimal. Flat-rate pricing lets you use the full duration without cost concerns.

### 3. Better for Scale
As video volume increases, the cost advantage of flat-rate pricing compounds significantly. What starts as a 95% savings per video becomes substantial at scale.
